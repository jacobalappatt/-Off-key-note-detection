Experiment 1

 

On half of the melodies there will be a mistake that occurs towards the end of the melody. The mistake will be an incorrect note substituted for the correct note.
You will answer 2 questions per melody;

 

1) Did the melody contain a mistake?
2) How familiar was the melody to you?

 

For the second question on familiarity, you should judge the familiarity of the musical style of the melody rather than the familiarity of the specific melody itself.
Please rate your familiarity on a scale of 1-5, 1 being the least familiar and 5 being the most familiar.
You are encouraged to use all five values of the scale over the course of the experiment
